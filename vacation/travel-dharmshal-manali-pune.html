Travel Plan (Route Options)

Pune couple + Indore couple — meet in the hills (or at a transit hub) and continue together.

Kid-friendly
Option A (Recommended): Pune → Dharamshala → Manali → Return Best balance

Fly in: to Kangra (Gaggal/DHM) → taxi to McLeod Ganj

• Pune → (transit) → Kangra (DHM)
• Indore → (transit) → Kangra (DHM)
Stay 3 nights in McLeod Ganj.

Hill transfer: Dharamshala → Manali (day drive)

Private cab is most comfortable for kids. Keep snack + restroom stops.

Return: Manali → Bhuntar (Kullu) Airport → Pune / Indore

Return via connecting flights. (Or do train-plan return.)

  • Stay split: 3 nights Dharamshala + 3 nights Manali + 1 buffer day
  • Best for kids: fewer hotel changes + daytime hill rides
Option B: Pune → Manali → Dharamshala → Return More road early

Fly to Bhuntar (Kullu) → road to Manali

• Pune/Indore → (transit) → Bhuntar → Manali (3 nights)

Manali → Dharamshala (day drive)

Then 3 nights Dharamshala. Return via Kangra (DHM) airport or train plan.

Budget alternative: train-based plan (details below)

Use train for long leg + cab in hills (cheaper than flights).

Tip: Keep a buffer half-day for weather/traffic

Train Travel Option

Best for budget control. Longer journey, but comfortable if you choose 3AC/2AC.

Train + Cab
Train Plan: Pune / Indore → Pathankot → Dharamshala → Manali Budget

Step 1: Train to Pathankot

Pune/Indore → Pathankot (long-distance). Choose 3AC/2AC for kids comfort.

Step 2: Pathankot → Dharamshala (cab)

After train arrival, take cab/bus up to Dharamshala / McLeod Ganj.

Step 3: Dharamshala → Manali (cab day drive)

Most family-friendly: daytime drive with snack + break stops.

  • Return: Manali → (cab) → Pathankot → Train back
  • Or mixed: Train one-way, flight one-way (time + budget balanced)
When train option is best Practical
  • When flights are too expensive for your dates
  • When you want predictable travel (less flight disruption)
  • When you can add 1 buffer day for long travel

Family tip

Carry: motion-sickness meds, snacks, light blanket, small pillow for kids.

Best Time to Travel (Months + Temperature)

Simple month windows so planning is easy.

Seasonal guide
Dharamshala / McLeod Ganj Pleasant
  • Best overall: March to June (daytime ~20°C to ~35°C)
  • Monsoon: July to September (cooler + lush, but rain/landslide risk)
  • Winter: October to February (cold nights; pack layers)
Tip: With kids, prefer March–June for comfortable day sightseeing.
Manali Snow season
  • Best for sightseeing: March to June (~10°C to ~25°C)
  • Best for snowfall: December to February (cold; roads can get blocked)
  • Monsoon: July to August (avoid if possible due to heavy rain + road risk)
Tip: If you go in peak snow months, keep a buffer day for traffic/road closures.
