Day 2 • Morjim → Parra Road → Candolim → Fort Aguada → Morjim

Parra Road + Candolim + Fort Aguada

This day combines the famous coconut-tree Parra Road, a relaxed beach stop, and the classic Fort Aguada view.

Route Overview

Start:
Living Room Beach Resort, Morjim
End:
Back to Morjim hotel

Plan for the Day

  • Leave after breakfast.
  • Short photo stop at Parra Road.
  • Continue to Candolim Beach.
  • Lunch nearby.
  • Visit Fort Aguada in the later afternoon.
  • Return to Morjim by evening.

Best Timing

  • Start: 8:00 AM
  • Parra Road: 9:00 AM to 9:30 AM
  • Candolim Beach: 10:30 AM to 12:30 PM
  • Lunch: 1:00 PM
  • Fort Aguada: 3:30 PM to 5:00 PM
Keep Parra Road as a quick stop only. It is best for photos, not a long visit.

28 March • South Goa Long Day
Approx 25°C–33°C
Day 4 • Morjim → Palolem → Butterfly Beach / Agonda → Morjim

Palolem Beach + Optional Butterfly Beach

A scenic South Goa day. Prioritize Palolem, and keep Butterfly Beach optional depending on weather, energy, and kids’ comfort.

Route Overview

Start:
Morjimbay Resort, Morjim
End:
Back to Morjimbay Resort, Morjim

Plan for the Day

  • Start early because travel is long.
  • Main stop: Palolem Beach.
  • Optional boat trip to Butterfly Beach.
  • Alternative quieter stop: Agonda Beach.
  • Return before it gets too late for kids.

Best Timing

  • Leave Morjim: 7:00 AM
  • Reach Palolem: around late morning
  • Beach time + lunch: till 2:00 PM
  • Optional Butterfly / Agonda stop: 2:30 PM to 4:00 PM
  • Return journey: early evening
This is the longest day. Keep snacks, water, extra clothes, and sun protection ready for kids.
