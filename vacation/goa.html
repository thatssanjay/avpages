Goa Family Trip Plan

Family of 6 • 2 Couples • 2 Kids • March 2026

Arrival Day
Day 1 • Arrival at Goa International Airport

Check-in + Relaxed Beach Evening

Arrive in Goa, transfer to your peaceful stay area, freshen up, and spend the evening with a light beach walk and family dinner.

Suggested Stay Area: Benaulim / Colva / South Goa for peaceful family stay, beach access, and relaxed vibe.

Plan for the Day

  • Pick up from airport by pre-booked cab or rental car.
  • Check-in and rest after travel.
  • Evening visit to nearby beach.
  • Keep first day very light for kids and elders.

Best Timing

  • Airport to hotel: based on arrival time
  • Beach walk: 5:00 PM to sunset
  • Dinner: 7:30 PM to 9:00 PM
Hotel Recommendation Budget target: Under ₹5K per room/night or a 2BHK family stay if available.
Focus on South Goa family hotels, apartments, or homestays near Benaulim / Colva.

Beach Day
Day 2 • South Goa

Benaulim + Colva + Peaceful Family Time

Enjoy a calm beach day in South Goa. Perfect for kids, photography, and relaxed family time without long travel.

Plan for the Day

  • Morning at Benaulim Beach for peaceful vibe.
  • Return to hotel for breakfast or brunch.
  • Rest during afternoon heat.
  • Evening visit to Colva Beach for family outing and food.

Best Timing

  • Benaulim Beach: 7:30 AM to 10:00 AM
  • Hotel rest: 12:00 PM to 4:00 PM
  • Colva Beach: 5:00 PM to 7:30 PM

Food Suggestion

  • Simple breakfast at hotel.
  • Lunch near hotel or indoor family restaurant.
  • Evening snacks near Colva beach.

Heritage Day
Day 3 • Panjim + Fontainhas

Fontainhas Heritage Walk + Panjim Sights

Explore the colorful Latin quarter of Goa, old Portuguese charm, local stories, and optional church visit.

Plan for the Day

  • Start early and drive to Panjim.
  • Take guided Fontainhas walk.
  • Visit Church of Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception.
  • Optional visit to Basilica of Bom Jesus if time allows.

Best Timing

  • Leave hotel: 8:00 AM
  • Heritage walk: 9:30 AM to 11:30 AM
  • Lunch in Panjim: 12:30 PM
  • Optional church visits: 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM
Commute Tip Use a full-day private cab for this day. It is the easiest with family and kids.

North Goa Day
Day 4 • North Goa Sightseeing

Candolim + Fort Aguada + Scenic Drive

A balanced North Goa day with famous beaches, fort views, and a family-friendly pace.

Plan for the Day

  • Leave early from hotel.
  • Visit Fort Aguada first to avoid crowd and heat.
  • Head to Candolim Beach for relaxed time.
  • Optional short scenic stop at Parra Road if you want photos.

Best Timing

  • Start from hotel: 7:30 AM
  • Fort Aguada: 9:00 AM
  • Candolim Beach / lunch: 11:30 AM to 2:00 PM
  • Return before late evening traffic

Family Tip

  • Do not overpack too many North Goa spots in one day.
  • Keep extra water, caps, sunscreen, and snacks for kids.

Scenic South Goa
Day 5 • Palolem + Butterfly Beach

Palolem Beach + Optional Butterfly Beach

A scenic South Goa day with one of the most beautiful beaches in Goa and optional boat trip if family is comfortable.

Plan for the Day

  • Start early and reach Palolem by morning.
  • Spend time at the beach and enjoy photography.
  • Optional boat ride to Butterfly Beach, depending on weather and kids’ comfort.
  • Keep evening relaxed and return early.

Best Timing

  • Leave hotel: 8:00 AM
  • Palolem Beach: 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM
  • Lunch nearby: 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM
  • Optional Butterfly Beach boat trip: 2:30 PM
Important Note Butterfly Beach may need a boat or trek. With kids, keep it optional and decide on the same day.

Departure Day
Day 6 • Return

Breakfast + Checkout + Airport Departure

Keep the last day stress-free. Pack early, have breakfast, and leave with enough travel buffer for airport.

Plan for the Day

  • Morning breakfast and final packing.
  • Keep 3 to 4 hours buffer before flight.
  • Leave hotel on time depending on airport location and traffic.
  • Carry water, snacks, and kids essentials separately.

Checklist Before Leaving

  • ID cards, tickets, hotel bills, medicines
  • Phone chargers, kids’ items, sunglasses, hats
  • Any local shopping packed separately
Final Travel Advice March end in Goa can be hot. Keep light cotton clothes, sunscreen, hats, slippers, and hydration ready throughout the trip.
