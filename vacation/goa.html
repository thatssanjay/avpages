Day-by-Day Itinerary

Hour-by-hour plan • Bold = Must Do • Optional = if time permits

Day 1 – Wednesday, 25 March 2026

Arrival Day · Morjim & North Goa · Hotel: Living Room Beach Resort

☀️ 34°C · Sunny & Clear · Best time out: 9 AM – 12 PM & 4:30 PM+
8:00 AM
✈️ Arrive at Goa Airport MUST
Flight lands around 8 AM. Collect luggage. Your Bharat Taxi 7-seater driver should be waiting at the arrivals gate. ~55 min drive to Morjim.
💡 Get your driver's number on Day 1 itself. Save it on all adult phones!
9:30 AM
🍽️ BREAKFAST: The Bodhi Tree Cafe, Morjim ⭐ 4.8
Goa's most beloved cafe! Famous for incredible momos, fresh juices, and Tibetan-Goan fusion breakfast. Warm, welcoming atmosphere. No hotel breakfast today.
🥭 Must try: Pineapple Juice, Steamed Momos, Eggs Benedict · Budget: Rs 300–400/head
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
10:30 AM
🏨 Check-in: Living Room Beach Resort MUST
Check in, freshen up. Early check-in possible if you call ahead. Leave luggage if room not ready and explore the beach!
11:30 AM
🐢 Morjim Beach – Olive Ridley Turtle Beach MUST
Goa's most peaceful beach! Famous protected nesting site for endangered Olive Ridley sea turtles. Kids love collecting shells and the calm, clean water. Also called "Little Russia" for its relaxed vibe.
🐚 Shell collecting for kids! Very shallow, safe water near shore. Avoid swimming area marked in red flags.
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
1:00 PM
🦐 LUNCH: Uma by the Sea, Morjim ⭐ 4.6
Spectacular beachfront restaurant at Morjim. Walk right on the beach at your table at night! Tables on sand in evenings – very romantic & family-friendly.
🍛 Must try: Chicken Xacuti, Goan Fish Curry & Rice, Kokum Shots, Prawn Masala · Rs 600–900/head
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
2:30 – 4:30 PM
😴 Hotel Rest / Pool Time REST
Peak heat 2–4 PM. Relax at hotel, swim in pool. Let kids nap – big days ahead! Never go out in afternoon sun without sunscreen.
4:30 PM
🏖️ Ashvem Beach (~4 km north of Morjim) MUST
One of Goa's most pristine and untouched beaches. Soft white sand, calm water. Very peaceful – mostly international tourists. Perfect for kids to swim safely. Beautiful for sunset walk.
👟 Sunset walk highly recommended! Bring towels. Less crowd than any other beach.
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
6:30 PM
🌿 Morjim Hill View Deck OPTIONAL
Small elevated deck with backwater and mangrove views. Short easy climb. Free entry. Best at sunset – beautiful golden hour views over the river.
📍 Map
7:30 PM
🍹 SUNDOWNER: Blue Turtle Beach Bar ⭐ 4.7
THE best sundowner spot in Morjim! Beachfront with sheesha, spectacular cocktails, and fresh seafood. Fairy lights, chill music, and sea breeze make it magical.
🥂 Must try: BBQ Salmon Tacos, Lamb Cutlets, Pina Colada, Sheesha · Rs 1000–1500/couple
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
9:30 PM
🌙 Back to Hotel – Rest
Early night after long travel day. Big sightseeing day ahead!

Day 2 – Thursday, 26 March 2026

Full North Goa · Heritage + Beaches + Markets · Hotel: Living Room Beach Resort

🌤️ 34°C · Light Breeze · Ideal for sightseeing!
7:30 AM
🌅 Mandrem Beach – Sunrise Walk MUST
Goa's most peaceful and cleanest beach. Only 8 km from Morjim. Shallowest, safest water in Goa for kids. Gorgeous sunrise. Long open beach – ideal for morning walk.
🌊 Kids can safely play in very shallow water. Lifeguards on duty. Clean & free of crowds.
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
9:00 AM
🥐 BREAKFAST: Local Cafe at Mandrem LOCAL GEM
Grab breakfast at one of the laid-back cafes near Mandrem Beach. No hotel breakfast today. Try authentic Goan street food!
🍞 Must try: Choriz Pav (Goan chorizo bread), fresh coconut water, Goan Poha · Rs 150–250/head
10:00 AM
🌳 Parra Road Drive – Instagram Famous! OPTIONAL
Beautiful tree-canopied road through old Goan village. Portuguese heritage homes on both sides. 15-minute photo stop. Great for family reels!
📸 Drive slowly – beautiful light through tree canopy. Best for morning photos.
📍 Map
10:30 AM
🎨 Fontainhas – Walk with Mario, Panaji MUST
Goa's iconic Latin Quarter! Book the famous "Walk with Mario" heritage tour. Colourful Portuguese houses painted in vibrant pastels. Incredible street art, history, and culture. Kids love the colours and art!
📞 Book Walk with Mario in advance: +91-9422435410 · Duration: 1.5 hrs · Rs 500/person
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
12:00 PM
⛪ Immaculate Conception Church, Panaji MUST
Goa's most photographed landmark! Stunning white baroque church built in 1541. Just 5 mins from Fontainhas. Best photo stop in all of Goa! The iconic white staircase is perfect for family photos.
📸 Best photo angle: from the bottom of the main staircase looking up. Morning light is perfect!
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
1:00 PM
🦀 LUNCH: Viva Panjim, Panaji ⭐ 4.4 · LEGEND
Hidden in the lanes of Fontainhas – the most authentic Goan restaurant in Panaji! Small, cozy, family-run. Legendary food – book or arrive early!
🍛 Must try: Sorpotel, Goan Vindaloo, Prawn Balchao, Fish Curry Rice, local Feni · Rs 500–700/head
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
2:30 PM
🛍️ Mapusa Market / Anjuna Flea Market OPTIONAL – IF OPEN
Local market for Goan spices, cashews, handicrafts, Feni, Goan sausages, and souvenirs. Busy but great fun! Kids enjoy the chaos and local vibe.
💰 Bargain at 50% of asking price! Carry cash. Best buys: Cashews, Feni, spice mixes, crochet items.
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
4:30 PM
🏖️ Candolim Beach MUST
One of Goa's cleanest and most refined beaches. Much less crowded than Calangute/Baga. Clean water, safe for swimming. Water sports available for adults.
🤿 Adults can try parasailing (~Rs 1000/person) or jet ski while kids play on shore with family member.
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
6:00 PM
🏰 Fort Aguada – Sunset MUST
Goa's most impressive 17th-century Portuguese fort! Stunning lighthouse and panoramic sea views. Sunset from the top is BREATHTAKING. Kids love climbing the fort walls. Free entry!
🌅 Go before 6:30 PM for sunset views. Carry water. Easy walk to top – kids manage well.
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
7:30 PM
🦞 DINNER: Calamari, Candolim ⭐ 4.5
Excellent beachfront restaurant famous for grilled seafood and Goan specialties. Great atmosphere with sea view dining.
🦐 Must try: Grilled Prawns, Goan Prawn Curry, Grilled Fish, Cocktails · Rs 800–1200/head
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map

Day 3 – Friday, 27 March 2026

Hotel Change Day · Checkout 11 AM → Check-in 2 PM · Casino & Baga Evening

☀️ 35°C · Humid – Stay very hydrated today!
⚠️ Hotel Change Today!
Checkout Living Room, Morjim by 11:00 AM · Drive ~45 mins · Check-in BloomSuites, Calangute at 2:00 PM · Gap = Calangute beach + lunch!
7:30 AM
🍳 BREAKFAST: Bodhi Tree Cafe, Morjim LAST MORJIM MORNING!
Last morning at Morjim! No hotel breakfast today. Head back to the beloved Bodhi Tree for your farewell Morjim meal.
☕ Try their full breakfast spread one last time. Order extra momos for kids!
9:00 AM
🌊 Final Morjim Beach + Pack & Checkout
One last morning walk on beautiful Morjim Beach. Collect shells for kids. Pack all bags. Checkout by 11:00 AM sharp.
11:45 AM
🏖️ Calangute Beach – Quick Arrival Visit MUST
India's most famous beach! Busy and buzzing with energy. Great for first impressions. Water sports available. Kids love the beach atmosphere and street food nearby.
🏄 Adults can try banana boat ride (Rs 250/person) or parasailing while older kids watch!
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
1:00 PM
🦀 LUNCH: Souza Lobo, Calangute ⭐ 4.2 · Est. 1932!
A Goa LEGEND! This iconic restaurant has been serving since 1932. Authentic, time-tested Goan recipes. Seafood here is outstanding. Beachfront location.
🦞 Must try: Crab Xacuti, Prawn Rissois, Fish Recheado, Goan Fish Curry · Rs 700–1000/head
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
2:00 PM
🏨 Check-in: BloomSuites Calangute CHECK-IN
Hotel 2 check-in at 2:00 PM. Freshen up and rest a bit. From tomorrow breakfast is included! This hotel is perfectly placed for Baga, Calangute, and casino visits.
3:30 PM / EVENING
🎰 Big Day Casino – Offshore Experience!
Goa's famous offshore casino experience on the Mandovi River! Entry includes food, drinks, and gaming credits. Adults only on gaming floor. Check if family lounge is available for kids to wait. Alternatively, plan this as an adults-only evening activity after kids sleep.
🎲 Adults Only Gaming 🍽️ Food Included 🥂 Drinks Included 💰 Entry: Rs 1500–2500 🚢 Boat to Casino
💡 Option A: Go at 3:30 PM (before other sightseeing) if kids are with a parent at hotel · Option B: Evening after kids sleep at hotel · Call ahead: +91-832-2243033
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
5:30 PM
🌊 Baga Beach – Evening Walk MUST
Goa's most iconic beach strip! Vibrant, colourful, full of energy. Street food, tuk-tuks, ice cream carts, live music. Kids love the evening atmosphere.
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
8:00 PM
🎸 DINNER: Britto's Beach Restaurant, Baga ⭐ 4.3 · LEGENDARY
Goa's most legendary beach restaurant! On Baga since forever. Sizzlers, fresh seafood, BBQ chicken, and live music most evenings. Extremely family-friendly!
🎵 Live music usually starts at 8 PM. Arrive a bit early for best beachside tables. Kids menus available!
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map

Day 4 – Saturday, 28 March 2026

Chapora Fort + Vagator · North Goa's Best · Hotel: BloomSuites · Breakfast Included ✅

🌤️ 34°C · Sea Breeze · Lovely beach day!
✅ Breakfast included at BloomSuites today!  |  🏖️ North Goa only – Palolem removed for less hassle with kids  |  More beach time & relaxation!
7:30 AM
🥐 BREAKFAST: BloomSuites Hotel – INCLUDED ✅
Enjoy your included hotel breakfast. Load up for a great day in North Goa!
8:30 AM
🐬 Dolphin Boat Trip from Sinquerim OPTIONAL – KIDS LOVE!
Early morning is the best time to spot dolphins! Short 45-min boat trip from Sinquerim jetty. Kids absolutely love dolphin watching! High success rate in March.
🐬 Book through hotel or any operator near Sinquerim Beach. ~Rs 400–600/person. Best before 9 AM!
10:30 AM
🏰 Chapora Fort – Dil Chahta Hai! MUST – ICONIC
Goa's most famous fort! Immortalised in the Bollywood film "Dil Chahta Hai". Dramatic views of the Arabian Sea and Vagator Beach. Free entry! Short easy climb – kids manage well. Panoramic views from all sides.
🎬 The exact spot where Aamir Khan, Saif Ali Khan & Akshaye Khanna sat is on the northern wall! Must recreate this photo!
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
12:00 PM
🌊 Vagator Beach – Red Cliffs MUST
Dramatic red laterite cliffs overlooking the sea. Small, intimate beach. One of Goa's most beautiful and photogenic spots. Little Vagator has a secret beach cove perfect for photos.
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
1:30 PM
🍝 LUNCH: Thalassa, Vagator ⭐ 4.5 · VIEWS!
Spectacular cliffside restaurant with stunning sea views! Greek-Mediterranean cuisine in Goa. Undoubtedly one of the best views while dining in all of Goa.
🫒 Try: Fresh mezze, grilled seafood, Greek salad, sangria · Slightly pricey but views are WORTH IT · Rs 1000–1400/head
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map
3:00 – 4:30 PM
😎 Hotel Pool / Beach Rest REST
Return to BloomSuites. Cool off in pool. Kids play. Recharge for evening Baga adventures!
4:30 PM
🛍️ Anjuna Flea Market (Wednesdays only) / Calangute Market OPTIONAL
Anjuna Flea Market runs on Wednesdays – Day 4 is Saturday so it won't be open. Visit the Calangute or Tibetan market instead! Otherwise, the Calangute local market has great shopping for souvenirs and Goan products.
💰 Best buys: Cashews (Rs 500–600/kg), Feni bottles, spices, beachwear, handmade jewellery.
7:30 PM
🌟 DINNER: A Reverie, Calangute ⭐ 4.5 · AWARD WINNING
The best fine dining restaurant in North Goa! Award-winning Goan fusion cuisine. Beautiful ambience. Perfect for a special family dinner on your last full night in Goa.
🌟 Must try: Their Goan-French fusion tasting menu, seafood specialties, innovative cocktails · Rs 1200–1800/head · BOOK IN ADVANCE!
TripAdvisor ↗ 🔍 Google 📍 Map

Day 5 – Sunday, 29 March 2026

Last Morning · Shopping · Checkout · Departure · Breakfast Included ✅

☀️ 34°C · Clear Skies · Happy journey home!
7:30 AM
🥐 BREAKFAST: BloomSuites Hotel – INCLUDED ✅
Final hotel breakfast. Load up! Pack bags after breakfast for smooth checkout.
9:00 AM
🛍️ Last-Minute Shopping – Calangute & Tibetan Market
Final shopping run! Cashews are cheapest here vs anywhere else. Pick up Feni, spices, Goan sausages for home. Tibetan market for clothes and jewellery.
🥜 Best buy: 1 kg cashews ~Rs 500 vacuum packed. Buy Urak (seasonal cashew spirit – only March–May!) if available.
11:00 AM
🍮 Sweet Stop – Pick up Bebinca & Dodol MUST BUY!
Pick up Goa's famous Bebinca (layered coconut-egg dessert) and Dodol (coconut-jaggery sweet) to carry home. Available at any confectionery in Calangute/Panaji.
11:30 AM
🏨 Checkout BloomSuites + Drive to Airport
Checkout and head to airport. Allow 60–75 min buffer from Calangute. Traffic can be unpredictable near airport.
✈️ Mopa Airport: ~45 min drive · Dabolim Airport: ~60 min drive. Depart hotel by 11:30 AM for most afternoon flights.
Onwards
✈️ Departure – Carry the Sunshine Home! 🌞
Pack: Feni bottles, Cashews, Bebinca, Spice mixes, Choriz sausages, and a million golden memories of Goa!

🏖️ Goa Travel Tips

Essential tips for a smooth, fun, and safe trip

💰
Money & Shopping
  • Carry cash always – many shacks don't accept UPI/cards
  • Bargain at markets – start at 50% of asking price
  • Keep Rs 100–200 change for parking, tips, coconut water
  • Best buys: Cashews (Rs 500/kg), Feni, spices, Bebinca
  • Don't buy cashews at airport – overpriced!
🕐
Timing & Weather
  • Leave hotel before 9 AM for all morning activities
  • Rest 1–4 PM (35°C peak heat – avoid going out!)
  • Evening activities from 4:30 PM onwards
  • SPF 50+ sunscreen – reapply every 2 hours!
  • Carry reusable water bottles – fill at hotel daily
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
For Kids in Goa
  • Mandrem & Ashvem safest for kids – shallowest water
  • Avoid Baga/Calangute for swimming – strong undertow
  • Carry ORS sachets, mosquito repellent, first aid kit
  • Banana boat rides & dolphin trips are kid favourites!
  • Kids under 5 usually free at tourist spots
  • Sun hats & UV protection swimwear are essential
🏖️
Beach Safety
  • 🟢 Green flag = safe swimming | 🟡 Yellow = caution
  • 🔴 Red flag = NO swimming under any circumstances
  • Swim only between coloured flags with lifeguards
  • Never leave valuables on beach unattended
  • Don't swim after heavy meals or alcohol
🚗
Transport Tips
  • Book Bharat Taxi full day (~Rs 2500–3000) vs per trip
  • Get driver's number on Day 1 – save on all phones
  • Ola/Uber also work in Goa as backup
  • Parking at popular spots: Rs 30–50/car
  • Download Google Maps offline before arriving
  • Never rent bikes/scooters without proper experience!
📋
General Must-Dos
  • Book popular restaurants in advance (A Reverie, Souza Lobo)
  • Pre-book Walk with Mario: +91-9422435410
  • Keep ID copies – originals in hotel safe
  • Dress modestly at churches & heritage sites
  • Carry insect repellent – evening beach mosquitoes!
  • Try local toddy shop (tadi bar) experience once!
